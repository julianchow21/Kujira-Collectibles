✦ Smart Add

Type or paste one item per line. The parser auto-detects slabs, sealed singles, and raw singles.
Price = cost. Every $X you enter is the cost price (what you paid). Market price and Carousell asking price are set later on the row itself - not at entry time.
Examples:
Eevee 173 EN NM $9 → raw single, cost S$9
Charmander 44 sealed EN $30 → sealed single, cost S$30
Eevee 173 TAG 10 cert L6786028 rank 10th $81 → slab, cost S$81
PSA 9 Bulbasaur 143 #119569490 $77 → slab, cost S$77
Supports: TAG/PSA/CGC grades, EN/JP/CN/ID languages, sealed keyword, qty (e.g. x3), cert numbers (#), ranks (1st/2nd…)
